Bike Tour: Schöneberg Coworking Spaces

details tba Friday, September 15 2023 – 4:00pm

It's the Berlin-Brandenburg Coworking Festival 2023 and you know what that means! It's time to discover what the Berlin coworking ecosystem has to offer! Join me (John from tuesday coworking) as we all get on on our bikes and find out what's behind the doors of four very different Schöneberg coworking spaces!

Duration: 2ish hours
Meeting point is Großbeerenstrasse 60

The spaces we'll visit:

  • 16:00: Uma Hub (Großbeerenstrasse 60)
  • 16:40: tuesday coworking (Belziger Strasse 71)
  • (17:00 if there's time: tuesday coworking, Feurigstrasse 51)
  • 17:20: District One
  • 17:50: Engelnest
The Coworking Festival's Closing Party takes place at Engelnest from 18:00, so why not stick around after the tour for some drinks and super casual networkin'!

Make sure to RSVP so we don't pedal off without you!
